    Sync across devices

    Everything already saves on this device. Sign in to also keep it synced to your phone or another computer, using your own free Firebase project — Tide never sends your data anywhere else.

    First time setting this up?

    See README.md in the project for the 5-minute, free, one-time Firebase setup. Until then, Tide works fully offline on this device — nothing is lost.

    Motivation photos

    Add your own images or gifs — after each timer session, one shows up at random to cheer you on. They're saved in this browser and, when you're signed in above, synced to your own private Firebase project (never part of the site's code, never visible to anyone else). Photos are compressed on upload to fit comfortably within Firebase's free tier; a photo too large to sync stays on this device and is marked "Local only".
